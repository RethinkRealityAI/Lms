### Slide 11 — rich_text + images

**type:** rich_text

**title:** Advocacy for Patients with Sickle Cell Disease

**content:**
Advocacy for patients with SCD is critical because health care teams largely lack knowledge about this condition and effective ways to treat it.

There is also a significant power imbalance built into the medical encounter.

![image](https://media.edapp.com/image/upload/v1708327046/applications/6417d1985247b69fb6c3c6dc/documents/1_q07ogu.png)

Patients attempting to advocate for themselves by requesting particular treatments or treatment steps, especially in the throes of a pain crisis or complication, may find that their attempts are met with negativity by the healthcare team and may also find themselves labelled accordingly as demanding, drug-seeking, bossy, or rude.

![image](https://media.edapp.com/image/upload/v1708327050/applications/6417d1985247b69fb6c3c6dc/documents/2_npog8t.png)

This only compounds patient distress and concern.
